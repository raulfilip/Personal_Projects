  The project's primary goal is to streamline the management of a football championship by effectively storing and swiftly accessing pertinent data in databases.
The key features of the project include:
Database Design and Management: Creating and overseeing a database tailored to the specific requirements of the competition.
Graphical User Interface (GUI): Developing a user-friendly interface to foster seamless interaction between end-users and the database.
Data Manipulation: Enabling users to perform essential operations such as adding, deleting, or modifying player, team, competition, and match information.
Real-Time Match Simulation: Implementing a feature for live match simulations, including scoring goals and applying player sanctions in real-time.
Automated Points Calculation: Instantly computing and displaying team points to determine their standings within the league.
