The object-oriented and java-based Order Management System is engineered to emulate the order creation process of an online shop.
This project incorporates the following pivotal features:
Product and Customer Management: Facilitates the addition and administration of products and customer profiles.
Order Creation: Empowers customers to place orders, and it supports the association of multiple orders with a single customer.
Database Integration: Utilizes a database to store product, customer, and order data, ensuring robust data management.
Invoice Generation: Automates the generation of invoices, streamlining the process of record-keeping and financial transactions.
In the realm of IT and e-commerce, this system serves as a valuable tool for managing online retail operations, 
enhancing efficiency in product and order management while maintaining accurate financial records.
